the history of torpid.com

October 1995 torpid.com created. "lazy.com" was taken, so we searched in a handy thesaurus (the one that came for free on my roomates NeXT cube) and found the word torpid. network access provided via 28.8 dialup PPP.
September 1996 torpid.com is stealthfully placed under desk at magnify research in order to leech T1 bandwidth.
October 1997 network access cut off by the evil bastards at network solutions because we forgot to pay our bill. emergency credit card payment via phone saves the day.
January 1998 server moved to alexa internet in sunny california due to job change. joy of sitting on a 12Mbps connection dampened by the fact that the web crawler and service uses up 11.5Mbps
February 1999 server moved to new hiding place at desktop. unwise coversion to linux from bsd facilitates hack attempts. torpid.com is twice compromised by root attacks
March 2000 server moved to a dirt cheap colo.
March 2002 To support the war on terror, alert status monitor added. Initialized to "Yellow" setting.
September 10, 2002 Moved alert status to "Orange" setting.
October 9, 2002 Belated return to "Yellow" setting.
January 28, 2003 Removed alert status meter. War on terror is over - replaced by war on Iraq.
October 16th, 2005 10 year anniversary of torpid.com, but nobody noticed until April 1st, 2006.
May 31st, 2010 torpid.com back online after long outage.
October 16th, 2010 15 year anniversary of torpid.com.
